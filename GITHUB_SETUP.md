## How to Apply These Settings

1. **Go to your GitHub repository**
2. **Click the gear icon (Settings) or "Edit repository details"**
3. **Add the description in the "Description" field**
4. **Add your website URL in the "Website" field**
5. **Add topics by typing them in the "Topics" field (separated by spaces)**
6. **Upload your social preview image in Settings > General > Social Preview**
